Description

This module implements a parser for the New Abbreviations. The purpose is to transform English text into a structured representation of a series of glyphs, which are the abbreviations themselves. Its intention is, insofar as the abbreviations are regular and systematic, to serve as a "reference implementation"; not to describe how they are rendered, of course, but to provide a reference for how any text should be abbreviated.
